## 🌟 Project Overview
This repository serves as an academic hub for research papers authored/co-authored by **Naina Morya**, a B-Tech CSE student and **MEXT Aspirant**. My work focuses on high-impact research areas:
- **Global Health Analytics:** Analyzing lifestyle and clinical factors for neurodegenerative risks in diverse populations (Japan vs. India).
- **Explainable AI (XAI):** Implementing SHAP and LIME to ensure clinical trust in automated diagnostic systems.
- **Aerospace Control Systems:** Designing PID controllers for aircraft stability in complex flight modes.
- **Medical Informatics:** Using Computer Vision and Big Data for disease detection.

## 📄 Research Papers

### 1. Alzheimer's Risk: Cross-Cultural Review (Japan/India)
**Title:** *Modular, Explainable, and Causal AI for Alzheimer’s Risk: A Cross-Cultural Review of Japanese and Indian Cohorts*  
**Publication:** IJNRD (Feb 2026)  
**Summary:**  
An in-depth review comparing lifestyle, behavioral, and clinical factors contributing to Alzheimer's in Asian populations. This paper proposes a modular learning framework to handle heterogeneous data across **Japan** and **India**, utilizing **Causal Inference** and **Explainable AI** (SHAP, LIME).
- **Key Findings:** Highlights roles of diet (fish/fiber), air pollution exposure, and social structures.
- **Strategic Focus:** Developing globally applicable medical AI models.

### 2. AsthmaXpert: Hybrid EAI System
**Title:** *AsthmaXpert: A Hybrid Explainable AI System For Personalized Asthma Risk Prediction*  
**Publication:** IJEDR (Sept 2025)  
**Summary:**  
Introduction of a hybrid ensemble model (Logistic Regression + Random Forest + Gradient Boosting) reaching **88% accuracy**. Utilizes **SHAP** for interpretability.
- **Deployment:** Streamlit-based web application for real-time clinician interaction.

### 3. Boeing 747-200 Stability Analysis
**Title:** *Analysis of Boeing 747-200 Longitudinal Stability Using PID Controller*  
**Publication:** IJCRT (June 2024)  
**Summary:**  
A mathematical study using transfer functions to evaluate aircraft longitudinal modes. Proposes a **PID Controller** design in MATLAB/Simulink to achieve stable flight characteristics for Alpha, Pitch, and Velocity modes.
- **Methodology:** Root Locus, Bode Plots, Step Response Analysis.

### 4. AI/ML in Aerospace Systems (Review)
**Title:** *Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in Aerospace Systems: A Comprehensive Review*  
**Publication:** JAAFR (Feb 2026)  
**Summary:**  
A thorough synthesis of AI/ML applications in aerospace, covering predictive maintenance, autonomous navigation, and satellite image classification. 
- **Core Models:** LSTMs, CNNs, DDPG (Reinforcement Learning).
- **Themes:** Explainable AI (XAI) and FAA/EASA certification standards.

### 5. Data Science in Medical Imaging
**Title:** *A Data Science Approach to Medical Imaging and Healthcare Data Analysis*  
**Publication:** IRJMETS (Oct 2024)  
**Summary:**  
Identifies optimal data science tools for analyzing clinical imagery (CT/MRI). Highlights the critical role of **OpenCV** in image processing and **PySpark** for integrating Electronic Health Records with genetic data.
- **Focus:** Oncology and Cardiology analytics.

### 6. Heart Disease Risk Assessment
**Title:** *Unveiling Key Clinical Indicators in Heart Disease: A Comprehensive Risk Assessment Framework*  
**Summary:**  
Investigates 11 clinical factors (age, cholesterol, ECG, etc.) using a dataset of 1190 instances. Identifies high-risk profiles through advanced statistical visualization in Python.
- **Insights:** Asymptomatic pain is a primary diagnostic symptom.
